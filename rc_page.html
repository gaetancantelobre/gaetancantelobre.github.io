RC/FPV Projects

This page is dedicated to all of my FPV/RC projects ! Here you can see what RC 'toys' I've been running with. I've been flying FPV since 2016. Mostly 5 inches quads, but as European regulations have become more strict most of my old quads have become illegal. So I've been moving to under 250g quads and other flying objects to stay legal. They are easy to transport as well. You can often see me on my campus flying in vacant parks as some FatShark goggles, a 3-inch quad or a whoop, a Radiomaster Zorro and some 2S batteries easily fit in my school bag.

My daily drivers

For all of my RC projects, I'm running a Radiomaster Zorro and a pair of FatShark Dominators V2 HD's.

B2 MINI's

I'm attempting 1h flight time under 100 grams check it out here: 1 hour flight time under 100g?

Two of my sub 100g delta wings. One of them is running 2S 450mAh LiPO's. And the other is running on a single 18650 3500 mAh Li-ion cell. The single-cell version part list:

  • FRAME : No name Aliexpress foam body
  • MOTORS : DYS 1104 - 7000Kv
  • ESC : 8A No name brushless ESC
  • RX: MATEK PWM ELRS 2.4G
  • PROPS : GEMFAN Hurricane 3018
  • CAM + VTX :25mw VTX + CAM COMBO
  • LIPO : 16850 Li-ion 3500mah 3.7v cell
  • SERVO : 2x 2g servos
  • EXTRAS : 3D printed parts (ex : motor mount)

The 2s version is exactly the same but with a 2s Li-Po 450mAh and a 15A ESC.

NBD Tiny Whoop

A sub 35g 1s tiny whoop with 31mm props. This thing is just too fun, super powerful, batteries are super cheap, fast to charge. This is the first "Bind'n Fly" quad and i think it will be my last...
First of all I enjoy building my quads but also because I had a hard time getting the built-in ELRS module to work.
And in the end i didn't, i had to by a new ELRS module to get this thing of the ground. The issue was due to the fact that the FC was sent with the wrong firmware.
Flashing a new firmware isn't an unknown task for me but the correct firmware was not available on their github, and tech support said it would be available in a couple months so...
I can fly this thing in my appartement (18mÂ²) and have fun. It's too light to hurt or damage anything or it's self.
Since the propellers and motors are in ducts, i don't have to worry about damaging the props, i got a bunch of spares but i can't imagine needing to change them any time soon!

NOOPY

A 64g (with a 450mAh 2S) 3-inch quad. With all the spare parts, a carry case, and 5 packs, we are at a wooping 240g. Easy to say that I have it on me all the time. (The carrying case is a McDonalds makeup bag) Fun little quad you can fly pretty much everywhere, the smaller quads seem to grow on me, so much more practical. Part list :

  • FRAME : Gems by Piratframes
  • MOTORS : EX1103 - 11000KV - Happymodel
  • FC + ESC + RX : AIO CrazyF405 ELRS HD 12A - Happymodel
  • PROPS : GEMFAN Hurricane 2023
  • VTX : BETAFPV M03 25mW 5.8G VTX
  • CAM :Caddx Ant Nano
  • LIPO :Tattu 2S 450mAh 75C
